About Me

Welcome to Coleman Adkins! A lifelong resident of Florida, Coleman was born one rainy morning in Hollywood in 1981. Since then, the rest is history. After graduating from the University of Central Florida in 2004 with a degree in Communications - Radio/TV , Coleman embarked on a wonderous journey into adulthood. Stints as a substitute teacher and Help Desk Technician left Coleman unsatisfied, so off to school he went again, once again studying Broadcasting at the luxurious Connecticut School of Broadcasting in Doral. As fate would have it, sports podcasting did not prove to be a lucrative venture for Coleman, which brings us to present day. Here you will find the genesis of Coleman's journey into Web Development. Enjoy!
